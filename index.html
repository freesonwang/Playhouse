Playhouse

PLAYHOUSE is a modern, simple, and elegant WebGL application. PLAYHOUSE is made with Javascript, using the MooTools framework, and KnockoutJS and WebGL libraries. Although, this started out as an exercise in learning WebGL, I decided to codify my learning a little more coherently into an actual application representing my vision for an easy-to-use, easy-to-learn, and pretty 3D creation application.

Comes with these great features:

  • Add and delete models from the stage!
  • Color your models!
  • Move your models!
  • Texture your models!
  • Basic lighting and shading is supported!
  • Load OBJ models from popular video games such as Final Fantasy, Portal, and Assassin's Creed!
